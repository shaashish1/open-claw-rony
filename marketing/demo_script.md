**Format:** Loom screen recording with face cam  
**Audience:** Indian SMB owners, founders, solo operators  
**Goal:** Drive bookings to https://cal.com/itgyani/15min

---

## Pre-Recording Checklist
- [ ] Browser: Chrome, clean profile, no distracting tabs
- [ ] Screen: 1920×1080 or 1440×900, 100% zoom
- [ ] Open tabs ready: itgyani.com dashboard, landing page, agent demo
- [ ] Mic: test audio — no echo, no background noise
- [ ] Face cam: well-lit, at eye level
- [ ] Water nearby (dry mouth kills pacing)

## Recording Tips
- **Scene 1:** Speak slowly — this is the hook. Don't rush.
- **Scene 2:** Use smooth, deliberate cursor movements. Avoid erratic scrolling.
- **Scene 3:** If live demo has a network delay, say "sometimes there's a small lag here — that's just my internet, not the agent." Keeps it real.
- **Scene 4:** Smile. The last 30 seconds should feel warm and inviting, not salesy.
- **Edit cut:** If Scene 2 runs long, cut the Support Agent section — 1 agent demo is enough.
- **Thumbnail:** Pause at 0:28 (just before dashboard) for a thumbnail frame with your face + the problem statement visible.
