FAQ

Questions? We Have Answers.

What exactly is an AI employee, and how is it different from a chatbot? +

A chatbot answers FAQs from a script. An AI employee is a fully autonomous agent that can research, write, send emails, update your CRM, book meetings, post content, and analyze data — all without human input. Think of it as a specialist who works 24/7 and never asks for a raise.

Do I need technical knowledge to use this? +

Zero technical knowledge required. We handle 100% of setup, training, and deployment. You access a simple dashboard to see what your agents are doing, review outputs, and approve actions. If you can use WhatsApp, you can manage your AI team.

What if the AI agent makes a mistake or sends something wrong? +

All agents have a human-in-the-loop approval layer for sensitive actions. You set thresholds — low-risk tasks run automatically, high-risk tasks wait for your approval. You are always in control.

How long does setup take, and what do you need from me? +

Typically 5–7 business days from discovery call to your agents going live. We need: a 30-minute onboarding call, access to your website, a product/service description, and your brand voice. We help create the brand voice guide if you don't have one. We handle everything else.

Is ₹999 really the full price? What's the catch? +

₹999 is the one-time setup fee — no catch. There's a monthly platform fee (₹999/month for Starter) covering hosting, compute, and maintenance. We're transparent because we want long-term clients, not one-time transactions. Cancel anytime.
